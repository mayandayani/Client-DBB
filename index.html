Services

Lash Lifting

A lash lift is a beauty treatment that enhances the natural lashes by lifting and curling them from the root.

It involves applying a special solution to the lashes, which helps to set them in a lifted position.

The result is longer-looking and more voluminous lashes that appear naturally curled. A lash lift can last for several weeks and is a great alternative to eyelash extensions for those who want a low-maintenance way to enhance their lashes.

$100

Lamination

Brow lamination is a beauty treatment that helps to shape and set the eyebrows in a desired position.

It involves applying a solution to the brows to relax the hair and then brushing them into place using a special technique.

The result is fuller, thicker-looking brows with a more defined shape. Brow lamination can last for up to 8 weeks and is a popular choice for those who want to achieve a more groomed and polished brow look.

$100
